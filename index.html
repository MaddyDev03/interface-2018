WHO WE ARE ?

A CULT OF TECHNO-ADDICTS WHO BELIEVE THAT ONE DAY 6*9=42

Abacus Club- The computer science society of St. Xavier's Sr. Sec. School, was founded in the year 1995. The Club is currently headed by Mrs. Charu Sood, H.O.D. of Computer Science Department and Mrs. Neelima Kamboj. We, comprise of die hard fanatics of technology whose passion for the technological science verges on borderline crazy. We comprise of 10+ brilliant (psychic) minds, each posessing a unique perspective of analysing the world around us.

"When all think alike, some bomb is bound to drop on our Campus."
We appreciate our achievements but never stop our crusade to acheive long lasting glory in the field of technological sciences. We have taken the competition scene in Delhi by storm, winning innummerable events but never letting it get in our way of learning.

The club comprises students of classes XI-XII, but all Xaverians are encouraged to learn something new, and build and ponder upon their skills. All the members of the club unite to contribute immensely to the technology environment in the school and actively take part in interschool competitions.
